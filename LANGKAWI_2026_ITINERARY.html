Arrive & Check In to Pelangi Resort
Check into Pelangi Beach Resort & Spa. Take a slow breakfast, unpack, and take your first look at Pantai Cenang. Resist the urge to rush — let the island pace set in.

Casual Cenang Lunch
Head along the Pantai Cenang strip for lunch. Try FatCUPID for local fusion (nyonya laksa, beef rendang) or grab nasi lemak from a roadside warung for an authentic first taste of Langkawi.

Beach Walk & Cenang Strip
Stroll the beachfront, browse duty-free shops along Cenang Road, or head 10 minutes south to quieter Pantai Tengah. Duty-free means affordable alcohol and chocolate — stock up if you'd like.
Pantai CenangDuty-Free ShoppingPantai Tengah

Golden Hour at the Beach
Return to the beachfront for drinks as the Andaman Sun drops. The Cliff Restaurant offers extraordinary sunset views over the sea — book ahead for a table at the edge.
The Cliff RestaurantBook ahead
🌙7:30 PM
Evening
Night Market or Seafood Dinner
If it's Thursday: the Temoyong night market at Pantai Cenang has 50+ stalls of ikan bakar, nasi lemak, satay, and tropical fruit. Otherwise: Orkid Ria Seafood for fresh lobster and black pepper tiger prawns under hanging lanterns.

Day 1 motto: Slow down. This day is intentionally easy. A rested first day makes Day 2's early start far more enjoyable — and you'll need it for the cable car.
DAY 2
SkyCab, SkyBridge & Mountain Views
Oriental Village
🌄8:00 AM
Early Morning
Early Start — Beat the Crowd
Leave Pelangi by 8am. Morning gives you the best light for photos, shorter queues at the SkyCab, and cooler temperatures. Drive or Grab to Oriental Village — approximately 20 minutes from Pantai Cenang.
Oriental Village~20 min driveLeave by 8am
🚡9:00 AM
Morning
Langkawi SkyCab
Board the SkyCab — one of the world's steepest cable cars — rising 708 metres above sea level on Machinchang mountain. Three stations: Pantai Kok Base, Middle Station, and Summit. Views begin before you reach the top.
Langkawi Cable Car708m summitBook tickets online
🌉10:30 AM
Late Morning
Langkawi SkyBridge Walk
Walk the world's longest curved suspension bridge — 125 metres, hanging above dense jungle canopy. The 360° panoramic views of the Andaman Sea, surrounding islands, and rainforest peaks are genuinely breathtaking. Don't rush this.
125m suspension bridge360° views
🍜12:30 PM
Midday
Lunch at Oriental Village
Return to Oriental Village's lakeside complex. Rest your legs and browse the colourful architecture blending Malay, Chinese, and Japanese styles. Several cafés and restaurants available — cold drinks are a priority after the altitude.
Oriental VillageLakeside dining
💧2:00 PM
Optional Add-On
Telaga Tujuh — Seven Wells Waterfall
Just 10 minutes from Oriental Village. A short jungle hike leads to seven cascading natural limestone pools. Excellent for a cool, peaceful swim surrounded by jungle. Easy for all fitness levels.
10 min from SkyCabSwimmingOptional
🏊4:00 PM
Afternoon Return
Back to Pelangi — Pool & Spa
Return to Pelangi by 4pm with daylight to spare. One last pool session, a spa treatment, or a peaceful final beach walk. Pack up and enjoy the slower pace before the farewell dinner.
Pelangi ResortPool / Spa
🦞7:00 PM
Farewell Dinner
Final Sunset Dinner
End the trip properly. Pia's The Padi for outstanding local cuisine against paddy field views, or Orkid Ria for a seafood feast. Toast to a perfect island escape with a duty-free sundowner on the beach.
Pia's The PadiOrkid RiaFarewell dinner
💡
Swap option: If you prefer sea over mountains, replace Day 2 with an island hopping tour to Dayang Bunting Lake and snorkelling on the Andaman. Book from Cenang Jetty the evening before — equally spectacular.
🚗 Getting Around
  • Grab works at Pantai Cenang & airport
  • Car rental: RM 70–120/day (most freedom)
  • Oriental Village: ~20 min from Pelangi
  • Taxis: unmetered — negotiate upfront
🍽️ Restaurants Quick Pick
  • Lunch Day 1: FatCUPID (Pantai Tengah)
  • Dinner Day 1: Orkid Ria / Night Market
  • Lunch Day 2: Oriental Village cafés
  • Dinner Day 2: Pia's The Padi
